The Best Movies According to Nusaif khan

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Transformers

It's a movie about The fate of humanity is at stake when two races of robots, the good Autobots and the villainous Decepticons, bring their war to Earth. The robots have the ability to change into different mechanical objects as they seek the key to ultimate power

Home Alone

It's a movie about an important message that family is what really matters to a child, or to anyone, and having them home for the holidays is better than wandering a huge house all by yourself.

Jumanji

Four teenagers are sucked into a magical video game, and the only way they can escape is to work together to finish the game
